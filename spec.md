



## callout

```
:::callout:warning{#id}
header (title)
***
body
***
footer (caption)
:::
```

- options:

  - info
  - warning
  - summary / tldr / abstract
  - todo
  - tip / hint
  - ...

  more ideas: https://notes.nicolevanderhoeven.com/Obsidian+Callouts
